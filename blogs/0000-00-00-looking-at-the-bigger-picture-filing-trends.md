Bankruptcy filings have been experiencing a double-digit year-over-year increase, but it's crucial to understand the broader context. During the pandemic, bankruptcy filings plummeted to historic lows, with 2020 seeing monthly declines over 40%. The current rise is more of a recovery to pre-pandemic levels.
